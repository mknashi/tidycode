Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

TL;DR: Your files, code, and documents stay on your device. We use privacy-friendly analytics (with your consent) to understand app usage, but we never see or collect your content.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tidy Code ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our online code editor and text editing application.

2. Your Content Stays Private

2.1 Local-First Architecture

Tidy Code is built with a privacy-first architecture. All your files, code, notes, and documents are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Code and Text Content: All files, notes, and text you create or edit
  • Todo Lists: Your task lists and completion status
  • Notes: Personal notes and annotations
  • User Preferences: Theme settings (dark/light mode), editor configurations
  • Tab State: Open tabs and their contents
  • CSV Data: CSV files and their edits

2.3 What We Never Collect

We do NOT have access to:

  • Your files, code, or document content
  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords or authentication data
  • The actual content you create or edit

3. Analytics (Optional, With Your Consent)

3.1 Why We Use Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use Tidy Code so we can improve the application. Analytics are only enabled if you give consent through our cookie consent banner.

3.2 What Analytics Collect

If you consent to analytics, we collect:

  • Usage patterns: Which features are used, page views
  • Device information: Browser type, screen size, operating system (anonymized)
  • Geographic region: Country-level location only (no city or precise location)
  • Session data: How long you use the app, bounce rate

3.3 Privacy Protections for Analytics

We configure Google Analytics with maximum privacy settings:

  • IP Anonymization: Your IP address is anonymized before storage
  • No Advertising: We disable all advertising features and signals
  • No Personalization: We disable ad personalization signals
  • No Cross-Site Tracking: We don't track you across other websites

3.4 Your Choice Matters

Analytics are completely optional:

  • You can decline analytics when first visiting the site
  • The app works fully without analytics enabled
  • You can change your preference by clearing your browser data and revisiting
  • Desktop app users are never tracked

4. How We Use Information

Your document content stays on your device and is never accessed by us. Analytics data (if consented) is used solely to:

  • Understand which features are most useful
  • Identify and fix usability issues
  • Prioritize future development
  • Understand our user base size and geography

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Browser Local Storage

All your application data (files, preferences, etc.) is stored using your browser's local storage API. This means:

  • Data persists between browser sessions
  • Data is only accessible from the same browser and device
  • Clearing browser data will delete all stored information
  • Other websites cannot access your Tidy Code data

5.2 Your Responsibility

As data is stored locally, you are responsible for:

  • Backing up important files (use download/export features)
  • Protecting your device and browser access
  • Managing browser storage settings

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Google Analytics

If you consent, we use Google Analytics 4 with privacy-focused settings. Google's privacy policy applies to their data processing: Google Privacy Policy

6.2 AI Features (Optional)

If you use AI features, your selected text may be sent to third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, or local Ollama) based on your configuration. This is user-initiated and requires explicit action.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Analytics cookie (optional): Only set if you consent to analytics
  • Consent preference: Stored in localStorage to remember your choice

We do not use cookies for advertising or third-party tracking purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Tidy Code does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we don't collect personal data and analytics are opt-in, the service is safe for users of all ages.

9. Data Retention

Your content: Remains in your browser's local storage until you delete it or clear browser data.

Analytics data: Google Analytics retains aggregated data according to their retention policies. We set retention to the minimum available period.

10. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the application
  • Export: Use download features to export your work
  • Delete: Clear data within the application or browser settings
  • Opt-out of analytics: Decline when prompted, or clear browser data to reset
  • Use desktop app: No analytics in the native desktop application

11. International Users

Your document content never leaves your device. If you consent to analytics, aggregated usage data may be processed by Google in the United States under their standard data protection measures.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. GDPR Compliance

Tidy Code is designed with GDPR compliance in mind:

  • Your document content is never collected or processed by us
  • Analytics require explicit opt-in consent
  • You can opt-out at any time
  • IP addresses are anonymized in analytics
  • No data is used for profiling or automated decision-making

14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific privacy rights. Tidy Code:

  • Does not sell personal information
  • Does not share personal information for targeted advertising
  • Provides opt-out controls for analytics

15. Security

While we don't store your content on servers, we recommend:

  • Using secure, updated browsers
  • Keeping your device secure
  • Regularly backing up important work
  • Using device encryption if available

16. Open Source Transparency

Tidy Code's code can be reviewed to verify our privacy claims. We believe in transparency and welcome security researchers to audit our application.

17. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or Tidy Code's privacy practices, you can contact us at:

Email: privacy@tidycode.ai
Website: https://tidycode.ai

18. Data Breach Notification

Since we don't store your document content on servers, the risk of a content data breach is eliminated. Your data security depends solely on your device and browser security. If any analytics data were compromised, we would notify users promptly.

19. Acceptance of This Policy

By using Tidy Code, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

Summary: Your files and documents stay on your device - always. We use optional, privacy-friendly analytics (with your consent) to improve the app, but we never see your content. You're in control.
